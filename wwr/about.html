History

Generations have enjoyed the sense of freedom with whitewater rafting.


Whitewater rafting has an honored history.

Harking back to the mid-19th century when explorers and adventurers,advanced the method of portaging in canoes to using early rubber rafts to navigate their surrounding rivers and rapids.

In the 1960s, whitewater rafting began its legacy as a recreational activity for everybody.

Today, RAFTING CRAFT honors this adventurous spirit by offering thrilling whitewater rafting experiences celebrating the sport’s storied past.
At RAFTING CRAFT, we believe in the power of adventure to bring families together, creating unforgettable memories as they navigate the rapids and enjoy the great outdoors.
Join us for a fun-filled, family-friendly rafting adventure that pays homage to the pioneers of whitewater rafting while fostering togetherness and joy.
